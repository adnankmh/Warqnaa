Integrated V0.2 Features
------------------------
- Dedicated translated "Daily Prize Box" page accessible from the home page.
- Six premium prize-box families with separate full/body/lid/front-panel assets.
- Front-opening five-second Flutter animation with shake, lid lift, front-panel movement,
  light effects, particles and reward reveal.
- One prize box awarded for each completed human-player win, capped at four boxes per day.
- Server-authoritative award/open flow with local offline fallback and idempotent win source keys.
- Reward pool: one-day original red Pasha, writing color, player color, profile cover,
  random tokens in multiples of 50 up to 1000, and a 200-value competition ticket.
- Temporary rewards are added to inventory with expiry timestamps and automatic expiry cleanup.
- Fourteen premium competition-ticket images with their value centered on the ticket.
- Original untinted red Pasha restored in the store, profile and reward system.
- Legacy Pasha color variants hidden from the user-facing store while retaining data compatibility.
- Universal administrator designer includes prize-box management and existing platform controls.
- Arabic, English, German, Turkish, French and Spanish V0.2 strings included.
