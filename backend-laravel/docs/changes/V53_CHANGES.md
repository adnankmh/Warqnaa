# Warqna v53 Pro - Full Gameplay Rules Expansion

## What changed

- Expanded active gameplay engines for all games currently available in Warqna.
- Added proper scoring/win/loss flow for:
  - Tarneeb, Tarneeb 400, Tarneeb 41
  - Estimation
  - Trix and Partner Trix
  - Hearts
  - Leekha
  - Baloot
  - Hokm
  - Kout 4 and Kout 6
  - Basra
  - Hand, Konkan, Banakil/Pinochle
  - Domino
  - Backgammon
  - Quick Training
- Added dedicated scoring helper for Baloot and meld games.
- Improved Baloot with Sun/Hokm card values, trump ranking, round points, and buyer success/failure.
- Improved Estimation with bids 0-13, exact-bid bonus, miss penalty, and winner by score.
- Improved Trix with contract scoring: King of Hearts, Queens, Diamonds, Hearts, Tricks, and Trix finish bonuses.
- Improved Hearts and Leekha with negative scoring and lowest-penalty winner.
- Improved Basra with same-rank capture, subset-sum capture, Basra table-cleaning bonus, last collector rule, and end scoring.
- Improved Domino side selection from the card/tile click.
- Updated the Games Rules catalog and translations for all supported languages.

## Important note

The rules are implemented as an original Warqna implementation based on common regional gameplay rules. No proprietary code, graphics, or protected assets from any competing platform are copied.
